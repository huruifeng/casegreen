About

What is this website?

hilites.today stands for highlights of the day today. It is a personal project I build for fun.
The original purpose is to create a space to share the trending videos, songs, and news of the day. After I submitted my I-485 application, I decided the build a website to track the progress of USCIS cases.

The data shown on this website is crawled from USCIS via the following link:
https://egov.uscis.gov/casestatus/mycasestatus.do?appReceiptNum=#{case_number}

Thank you to all the friends for your support. That keeps the website running on AWS. As I enabled Google Adsense, I removed PayPal donation link from main pages.
